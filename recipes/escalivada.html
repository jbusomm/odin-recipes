Escalivada

Description

Escalivada is a grilled vegetable dish from Catalonia, Spain, that's simple and flavorful.

It's usually cooked over hot charcoal until the vegetables are tender, sweet, and smokey, but it can also be done inside the house using the broiler in the oven (griller).

Similar to ratatouille, escalivada is a delicious, easy-to-make recipe that's sure to become a new comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 2 bell peppers
  • 1 medium egg plant
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 splash of Sherry vinegar
  • 1 piece of bread, for serving

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350ºF or 175ºC.
  2. Wash and dry your vegetable.s
  3. Rub them with olive oi.l
  4. Sprinkle them with a few pinches of salt.
  5. Wrap them in tin foil.
  6. Place them on a baking sheet, and roast them for 2 hours.
  7. Remove them from the oven and let cool.
  8. Peel the skins off the eggplant and the peppers.
  9. Slice your vegetables into 1/2 to 1 inch slices.
  10. Add a pinch of salt, drizzle with generous oil and add a big splash of sherry vinegar
  11. Serve with bread, cheese, meat, fish...
