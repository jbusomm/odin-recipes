Crema Catalana

Description

One of the most traditional Christmas recipes in Catalonia are canelons, also known as the Spanish style canelloni, locals usually eat them during boxing day.

Traditionally prepared with the leftovers of Christmas' eve dinner which consists of Escudella and Carn d'Olla, two dishes which are ¨all sort of meats¨ abundant and cooked with potatoes and chickpeas.

An aspect that differentiate this Spanish food recipe to the traditional one, mostly known as canelloni. The canelons recipe is eaten by the locals in Barcelona.

Ingredients

For stuffing

  • 18 cannelloni wrappers or 3 sheets fresh pasta, cut into 18 5" x 6" rectangles
  • 500 gr veal shank
  • 250 gr pork loin
  • 200 gr Iberian bacon
  • 1 organic chicken
  • 100 gr foie
  • 2 carrots and 2 tomatoes peeled and cut
  • 4 onions
  • 1 leek
  • 1 garlic head
  • 50 cl cognac
  • Olive oil

Bechamel

  • 70gr of butter
  • 70gr of flour
  • 1L of milk
  • Salt, pepper, nutmeg
  • Parmesan cheese (grated)

Steps

  1. Put all the ingredients in a tray except the foie.
  2. Rub them with salt and pepper.
  3. Sprinkle olive oil and cognac.
  4. Cover the tray with aluminum foil.
  5. Put int the oven for 3h at 140ºC (284ºF).
  6. Remove from the oven and mince all the ingredients together.
  7. Collect the fat and use it to fry the foie.
  8. Mix everything.
  9. Prepare the béchamel by melting butter in a saucepan.
  10. Stir in an equal amount of flour and cook the mixture for just under a minute.
  11. Stir milk, a little at a time, avoiding lumps to form.
  12. Stir the mix constantly bringing it to boil, so it thickens and becomes glossy.
  13. Season well with salt, pepper and nutmeg.
  14. Prepare the tray for the canelons, use oil from the mix to cover the base or just olive oil
  15. Stuff the canelons with the minced meat and lay them down in the tray
  16. Add the bechamel sauce on top of all canelons and sprinkle with grated truffle and parmesan cheese.
  17. Place the tray with the canelons into the pre-heated oven (190ºC/375ºF) and cook au gratin for 10 minutes, or the time needed for the cheese to melt and become golden brown.
  18. Et voila, your delicious canelons recipe is ready.
